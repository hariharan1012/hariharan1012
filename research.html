ECommerce and retail

The Challenge

The client is a leading retail company based in North America, that primarily deals in aftermarket automotive parts and accessories. They are currently revamping their website and making major changes to their business model. Current website does not yield to current market trends, navigation is very complex and hard to understand, bounce rate was at an increase.

My Approach

Current website does not yield to current market trendsThe first step was to identify where our client was lagging. Post this, I proceeded to understand current market trends by referring to several articles and concised my findings. The next phase was to dive and understand the type of customers that existed and what they preferred.

Insight and Next Step

For this process, I referred to several articles and also sent over a questionnaire to the onsite team to understand the user groups and demographs. Enterprise Medical System

The Challenge

As most enterprise system goes, different aspects of associate medical care were being handled by different teams such as Claims, Insurance, Fitness, etc. This meant that associates had to navigate to between different apps to perform the function they wanted. The second part to this application was to empower the organization to connect with the associates in a more personal level. To understand this better, we performed user interviews with different associates(long term employees and freshers) and the pain points we identifed in the process were:

  • No single point dedicated system for associates to handle their medical information
  • An incoherent method of organizing health events with employees
  • No level of personalization

My Approach

Post interviewing users, we began to compile the various features that users expected and classify them. Based on that we were able to arrive at our Information Architecture. Post framing the IA, we started to conceptualize our vision. Post a few iterations, we were able to decide on our final design.

The Design

Our design was to ensure that every associate is able to get access to their most wanted information as easily with as less clicks as possible along with a few additions to boost the usage of the app. We wanted an application that will not only help associates monitor the status of their claims or reimbursment but will also enable the enterprise to get a more personal touch with the associate. Features such as: Health Programs, Diet tips and Fitness tracker.

The Outcome

We proposed our design to a select group of associates and asked for a feedback. Every associate found the idea interesting along with a few suggestions of how they would like to personalize the application more to them. With the necessary changes made we p resented our pitch to the Leadership who appreciated our efforts. This application is being currently developed.
