About

I am passionate about User Experience and Design Thinking. My philosophy to UX is to keep things “simple, meaningful and impactful”. Right from my childhood I was an avid photographer / videographer and applied creative techniques when shooting my subjects to bring out unique experiences and evoke immersive emotions. Over the last three years I have been immersed in UX research and design programs at Cognizant working with exciting multi-national clients. I have gained significant professional experience to further hone my skills in UX. My approach to client engagements in UX is simply to:

  1. Engage with the client to connect and understand the problem
  2. Define the client’s vision, objectives and requirements in clear, simple language
  3. Understanding, de-constructing and re-constructing “simplified” user-centered process (use cases)
  4. Instill the creative process to build UX models based on optimal use-cases and clean design
  5. Ensure that the designs for the client are coherent and scaleable.

Through the course of my professional experience, I have been engaged in challenging assignments to architect high productive user interfaces for customers in the USA and APAC regions leveraging technologies like Pentaho, .net, Javascript and Bootstrap.
